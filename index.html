York University, Ontario, CA

I am a Research Assistant at BioMotion Lab in Toronto, Canada, working on Deep Learning models with applications in Computer Vision.

I am also a Master's student in the Electrical Engineering & Computer Science Department at York University, supervised by Prof. Nikolaus Troje and co-supervised by Marcus A. Brubaker. My Master's research focuses on developing Deep Learning models to generate novel face images from different views. My research also consists of Computer Vision/Graphic applications in Deep Learning. Previously I have done model/color recognition in my undergrad as my thesis project.

Before I joined BioMotion Lab, I graduated with an B.Sc. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University Of Tehran, Iran. In my Undergrad's thesis I used Machine Learning methods to indetify Car Specifications such as model & colour during day/night and different weather. During that time I was supervised by Dr. Reshad Hoesseini.
